Learn About Government Bonds

Government bonds are debt securities issued by the government to support government spending and obligations. They are considered low-risk investments. The government guarantees to pay the principal along with interest over the life of the bond.

Here's a breakdown of the bond terms available:

  • 2 Year Bond: 16.5% annual interest
  • 3 Year Bond: 20% annual interest
  • 5 Year Bond: 20.8% annual interest
  • 7 Year Bond: 21.5% annual interest
  • 10 Year Bond: 22.5% annual interest
  • 15 Year Bond: 23.5% annual interest
